Hair Dye

Hair Dye, or hair dyeing, is the practice of changing the hair color. The main reasons for this are cosmetic to cover gray or white hair, to change to a color regarded as more fashionable or desirable, or to restore the original hair color after it has been discolored by hairdressing processes or sun bleaching.
